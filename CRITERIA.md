

# Spaceship game

* There are planets randomly generated
* There are two spaceships
* You can shoot at each other, choosing direction and power
* Shots curve around planets with gravity
* If your shot hits a planet, your turn is over and the shot is destroyed
* Ships explode when shot and the survivor gets one point
* There is a time limit on shots, after which it explodes on its own with no points
* It’s turn based
* Each player has a name
* There’s a score board, best of three? 
* A backend where you can join games 
* It needs to not crash
